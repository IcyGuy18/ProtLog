PTMKB is designed to serve any and all data related to the Post-Translational Modifications (PTMs) of proteins. We aim to assist researchers and developers alike to advance in the understanding, exploration, and application of PTMs in cellular process. By providing simple access to a rich repository of PTM data, algorithmic calculations, and tools for integration into systems, we plan to accelerate breakthroughs in fields such as drug discovery, disease research, and biotechnology.

Get started with the website's help page by clicking here!

Key Features

Search and find extensive detail on PTMs (including evidences, secondary structures, and solvent accessible surface area)
Use the Propensity Calculator to find the occurrence of a PTM on a residue
Integrate PTMKB into systems through a fully-featured RESTful API for seamless data access and automation
View and download the Propensity matrices for inclusion in systems/scientific literature
